Privacy Policy - Chymia

1. Introduction
Chymia is an interactive chemical visualization tool designed to work within the ChatGPT environment. Our priority is to provide a safe and seamless educational experience while fully respecting your privacy.

2. What we DO NOT collect
To ensure your privacy and security from the ground up ("Privacy by Design"), our application operates entirely without user accounts. Therefore, we do not collect, request, or store:

Personally Identifiable Information (PII), such as your name, email address, or contact details.

Conversation history, prompt texts, or any messages exchanged between you and ChatGPT.

Passwords or authentication data.

3. What we DO collect (Telemetry and Usage Data)
To understand how the simulator is used and to fix potential interface issues, we collect anonymous interaction data through the PostHog analytics service. This exclusively includes:

Interface interactions: Clicks on specific simulator buttons (e.g., toggling the X-Ray view, pausing/resuming the simulation, changing speed, or selecting a chemical element).

Technical session data: An anonymous and temporary session identifier generated automatically to group interactions from the same session, without any link to your real identity.

4. How we use the information
The technical and interaction data we collect is strictly used to:

Identify which features (such as states of matter or chemical reactions) are most utilized, helping us prioritize future improvements.

Diagnose and fix bugs within the widget's graphical interface.

Improve the overall performance and stability of the simulator.

5. Data Sharing
We do not sell, rent, or monetize your usage data. All anonymous metrics are processed exclusively by our analytics provider (PostHog), which operates under strict security standards and complies with the requirements of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

6. Retention and Security
Because we do not maintain our own database to store user profiles, telemetry events are retained in PostHog only for the time necessary to conduct performance analysis. Afterward, they are irreversibly discarded or aggregated in accordance with the standard retention policies of the analytics platform. Furthermore, the simulator widget operates within a secure, sandboxed environment (iframe) enforced by ChatGPT.

7. Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how Chymia works under the hood, please feel free to reach out through the official project repository or the support channels provided by the developer.
